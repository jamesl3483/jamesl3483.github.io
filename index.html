Relavent Experience and Programs

  • NASA MINDS Competition

    Position: Programming Team Lead

    NASA MINDS is a design competition where students design solution to problems on the Artemis Mission. Our group chose decentralized swarm robotics and search algorithms. Due to budgeting concerns, We were combining Raspberry Pi, BrickPi, and Grove Sensors, which made so many problems that I had to work out along with my team. In the end, the project was stalled by constant hardware problems and we could only do basic collision avoidance. However, I learned a lot about navigating through Linux and using it to try mashing different systems and components together from having to constantly fix the hardware problems
